MindMeld - Learn Level {{ level }}

How to Play Level {{ level }}

{% if level <= 3 %}

Welcome to Path Finder! Guide the robot (🤖) to the flag (🏁) by clicking adjacent cells. Avoid skulls (💀) and reach the goal within the move limit.

{% elif level <= 6 %}

Welcome to Shape Sorter! Drag shapes from the left to matching slots on the right. Sort all pairs to win!

{% elif level <= 9 %}

Welcome to Number Crunch! Click numbers to add up to the target sum. Pick wisely—you don’t need all the tiles!

{% elif level <= 12 %}

Welcome to Word Weaver! Guess the hidden word one letter at a time, like Hangman. You have 6 wrong guesses before you lose. Use the hint button in the game to help!

Example: For "PYTHON", guess "P" → "P _ _ _ _ _", then "Y" → "P Y _ _ _ _". Keep going until you solve it!

{% elif level <= 15 %}

Welcome to Switch Swap! Click a switch to flip it and its neighbors. Turn all lights on (💡) to win!

{% elif level <= 18 %}

Welcome to Treasure Tap! Click tiles to find treasures (🏴‍☠) and avoid skulls (💀). Find all treasures within your taps!

{% elif level <= 21 %}

Welcome to Unscramble Words! Click letters to form the hidden phrase. Solve it before time runs out!

{% elif level <= 24 %}

Welcome to Pattern Recognition! Study the number sequence and pick the next number from the options.

{% elif level <= 27 %}

Welcome to Sequencing! Study the alphanumeric sequence and click the tiles in the correct order to match the pattern.

{% elif level <= 30 %}

Welcome to Riddles! Read the riddle and choose the correct answer from the options.

{% endif %}
